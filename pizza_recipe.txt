PIZZA RECIPE

8 Pound Batch of Dough
 ==============
Mix liquid in a 2.5-quart bowl:
	1  tablespoon Active Dry Yeast (or one packet)
	2  tablespoons Salt
	6¼ cups water
	¼  cup Oil

Dump 5-pound bag of flour on counter.
Fix in liquid.
Kneed until smooth.
Make eight dough balls, about 16 oz each.

STONE PIZZA
 ----------
preheat oven to 550 degrees F.
make pizza on stone.
bake for 9 minutes.

enjoy !
